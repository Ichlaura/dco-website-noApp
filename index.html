STANDARD WEB VERIFICATION – $4.60

Online insurance verification with a structured clinical breakdown.

Includes:

  • ✓ Eligibility, deductible, and annual maximum
  • ✓ Amount already used from annual maximum
  • ✓ Whether deductible has been met or partially paid
  • ✓ Preventive, Basic, and Major coverage overview
  • ✓ Waiting periods (general and category-specific)
  • ✓ Missing tooth clause
  • ✓ Downgrades (composite resins, crowns, bridges)
  • ✓ Preventive treatment history
  • ✓ Age limitations (fluoride, sealants, space maintainers)
  • ✓ Orthodontic details: whether orthodontics is covered, maximum age limit for orthodontic coverage, and whether coverage applies to adults
  • ✓ SRP same-day quadrant allowance
  • ✓ Crown payment timing (prep date vs seat date)
  • ✓ General coverage overview (periodontics, endodontics, extractions, crowns, bridges, dentures)
  • ✓ Plan notes, limitations, and exclusions
  • ✓ Structured format for easy clinical interpretation

All information is entered into the clinical system for record accuracy and workflow use.

STANDARD PHONE VERIFICATION – $6.00

Manual verification performed directly with the insurance carrier.

Includes all services in Standard Web Verification, plus:

  • ✓ Exact procedure codes (when available)
  • ✓ Detailed frequency breakdowns (crowns, bridges, bone grafts, etc.)
  • ✓ Adjunctive General Services details
  • ✓ Expanded periodontal, endodontic, and oral surgery coverage
  • ✓ Detailed orthodontic verification (extended carrier confirmation)
  • ✓ Detailed plan limitations and exclusions
  • ✓ Coverage conditions affecting approval
  • ✓ Enhanced clinical and case acceptance notes

This service is used when online verification is not available or incomplete.

Predetermination Support

+

We provide the clinic with a structured Excel form that must be completed with basic patient and insurance information for each predetermination request. The process is simple, fast, and highly efficient to complete.

All submissions are reviewed twice per week, ensuring consistent processing and timely updates.

Each case is categorized into:

Accepted

Denied

Pending

This allows your team to easily organize patient follow-ups and schedule appointments accordingly.
